MADURAI Biryani as temple prasadam: an 83-year-old tradition Vadakkampatti, a small village in Thirumangalam taluk of Tamil Nadu’s Madurai district, hosts an unusual annual temple festival. Where else can you find biryani served as prasad? And, it’s not just for devotees; anyone who walks into Vadakkampatti on January 25 this year will be treated to a hearty meal of mutton biryani. This has been a tradition for 83 years.
